Help & Feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vaccination appointment?
Go to the Home tab and tap "Vaccine Booking" or "Book OPD Token". Enter your Aadhaar number (it will auto-fill your details), select a center, vaccine type, date and time, then confirm your booking. You'll receive a QR code for your appointment.
What is ABHA and why do I need it?
ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is your unique digital health ID. It links all your health records across hospitals and lets you share them securely. Create one using your Aadhaar number in the ABHA tab.
Are vaccines safe? What are the side effects?
All vaccines on Swasthya Setu (Covishield, Covaxin, Corbevax) are WHO-approved and safe. Common mild side effects include soreness at the injection site, low-grade fever, and fatigue for 1-2 days. Serious side effects are extremely rare.
How does cold-chain tracking work?
Swasthya Setu uses IoT sensors and blockchain to track vaccine temperature (2-8°C) from manufacturer to your arm. Every temperature reading is recorded on an immutable ledger, ensuring your vaccine was stored safely throughout transit.
How do I track my vaccination records?
Go to the Records tab. You'll see your vaccination history, including vaccine type, date, and status. You can also access your records through the Secret Vault for secure storage.
Can I book for my family members?
Yes! Go to Profile → Add Family to add your family members with their Aadhaar numbers. Once added, you can book appointments for them using their details.

Security & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 January 2026

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: Name, Aadhaar number, date of birth, gender, phone number, address (via Aadhaar auto-lookup for ABHA creation).

Health Data: Vaccination records, appointment history, vitals (temperature, blood pressure, heart rate), eligibility assessments.

Usage Data: App interactions, language preferences, device information.

2. How We Use Your Data

To create and manage your ABHA Health ID, book vaccination appointments, track vaccine cold-chain integrity, provide AI-assisted health guidance, and generate health insights.

3. Data Security

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Vaccination certificates are stored in a PIN-protected Secret Vault on your device. Blockchain records are immutable and tamper-proof. We follow ABDM security guidelines.

4. Data Sharing

Your health data is never shared without your explicit consent. Data may be shared with: (a) ABDM-linked healthcare providers (with your consent), (b) Government health authorities (anonymized, for public health surveillance), (c) Insurance providers (only with your explicit authorization).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, withdraw consent, request data deletion, and port your data to other ABDM-compliant platforms.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies for session management. No third-party tracking or advertising is used.

7. Children's Privacy

Vaccination records for children under 18 are managed by their parents/guardians through the Family Members feature.
